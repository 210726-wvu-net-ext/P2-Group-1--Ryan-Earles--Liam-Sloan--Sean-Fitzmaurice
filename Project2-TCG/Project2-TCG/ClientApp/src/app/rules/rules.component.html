1. A game is played with a deck of 10 cards
2. A game is limited at 12 turns
3. Each turn you draw a card and gain 1 mana
4. When a player plays a card, the cost is subtracted from their mana pool, they may only play a card if they have enough mana, only one card in play at a time
5. Once a card is in play it can take two actions, directly attacking the opponent, or attacking the opponents card
6. Attacking the opponent directly sacrifices the card, and it is taken out of play
7. If the player or opponents health is taken below 0, the game ends
8. If neither the player or opponent's health is below 0 by the end of 12 turns, the winner is the side with more health, a draw is declared if they have the same amount
